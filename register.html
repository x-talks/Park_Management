Terms of Agreement

Parking Area Usage Agreement

By registering and using this parking management system, you agree to the following:

  1. Your assigned parking spot is for exclusive personal use. Subletting or sharing is not permitted.
  2. You are responsible for any damage caused by your vehicle to the parking area or other vehicles.
  3. Parking is only permitted within the boundaries of your assigned spot.
  4. Your contact details (name, phone, license plate, car model and color) will be visible to other registered residents to facilitate communication in emergencies.
  5. Changes to your personal data require administrator approval.
  6. Your account access is non-transferable and tied to your license plate as your username.
  7. The administrator may revoke access at any time in case of violations.
  8. You agree to keep your contact information up to date.
  9. Monthly rent: The monthly parking fee is due on the 1st of each month. If you register in the first 10 days of the month, the full monthly fee applies for that month. If you register between days 11–20, half the monthly fee applies. If you register after day 20, one third of the monthly fee applies for that month.

This agreement is governed by the house rules of the property.
