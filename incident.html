📷 How to take a good evidence photo
  1. License plate clearly visible — get close enough that the plate is readable
  2. Include the parking spot number — step back to show the spot marking on the ground or wall sign
  3. Good lighting — avoid strong backlighting; if dark, use flash
  4. Straight angle — shoot from the front or rear of the car, not a steep side angle
  5. One photo = one incident — capture everything in one clear frame if possible
